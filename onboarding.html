Where Ageinx sends users after a successful login. Must be http/https.

What is a slug? It's the unique identifier for your app's hosted login page — e.g. ageinx.vercel.app/auth/my-awesome-app. Your users will visit this URL to sign in or create accounts.

What is the callback URL? After a user logs in, Ageinx redirects them here with their auth token. This should be a page in your own app that handles the token.
